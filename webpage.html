Shubman Gill

Shubman Gill is an indian international cricketer who plays for the indian national team and currently is the captain of Team India in Test and ODIs and also the captain of The Gujrat Titans in the IPL.Shubman Gill has also captained India in T20.He is an incredible and a very good Batsman who has scored many centuries in Test and ODI Series.He also hold the record of the Youngest cricketer to score a double century in ODIs at the age of 23.
